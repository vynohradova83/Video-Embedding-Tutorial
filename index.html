How to Embed a YouTube Video Into a Website

This tutorial explains how to embed a YouTube video into a web page.

Step 1: Open a YouTube Video

Go to YouTube and open the video you would like to embed.

Step 2: Click Share

Under the video, click the Share button.

Step 3: Select Embed

Choose the Embed option from the sharing menu.

p>

Step 5: Paste the Code Into HTML

Paste the iframe code where you want the video to appear on your page.
